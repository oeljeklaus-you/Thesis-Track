Thesis Track

Record your investment reasoning, review real outcomes, and make clearer decisions next time.

Reason · Risk · Review

Investment decisions deserve a memory.

Thesis Track is a personal investment thesis journal for recording why you made an investment decision, what assumptions you had, what risks you saw, and what you learned after the outcome became clear.

Instead of only tracking price changes, Thesis Track helps you keep the reasoning behind each investment in one place.

What you can track

  • Investment thesis and one-sentence reasoning
  • Core assumptions, risks, and invalidating conditions
  • Review dates and post-exit reviews
  • Position plans, account notes, and operation records
  • Recurring decision patterns after multiple reviews
  • Common U.S. stock and ETF symbols with local search

Built for reflection, not recommendations

Thesis Track is designed for personal journaling, learning, and review. It helps you reflect on your own investment thinking over time.

Thesis Track does not provide financial advice, investment recommendations, price predictions, guaranteed returns, trading signals, or automated trading services. Investing involves risk. Please make your own decisions.
