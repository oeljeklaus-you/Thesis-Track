What is Thesis Track?

Thesis Track is a personal investment thesis journal. It helps users record their own investment reasoning, risks, review dates, and review notes.

Common questions

  • Does Thesis Track provide investment advice?
    No. Thesis Track is only for personal journaling and review.
  • Does the app provide buy or sell signals?
    No. It does not provide trading signals, recommendations, or price predictions.
  • Do I need an account?
    No account is required in the current version.
  • Where is my data stored?
    In the current version, records are stored locally on your device.
  • Can I use review reminders?
    Yes. If you enable notifications, Thesis Track can remind you when an investment thesis is due for review.

Disclaimer

Thesis Track is for personal investment journaling, learning, and review only. It does not provide financial advice, investment recommendations, price predictions, guaranteed returns, trading signals, or automated trading services. Investing involves risk. Please make your own decisions.
