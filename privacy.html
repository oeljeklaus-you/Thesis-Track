Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Thesis Track handles information. Thesis Track is a personal investment thesis journal designed for recording investment reasoning, risks, review dates, and review notes.

1. Information We Collect

In the current version, Thesis Track does not require an account, login, or registration.

The app is designed to store your investment records locally on your device. These records may include information you manually enter, such as:

  • Asset names and symbols
  • Investment thesis notes
  • Risks, assumptions, and invalidating conditions
  • Review dates and review notes
  • Position or operation records you choose to enter

2. Local Storage

Your records are stored locally on your device in the current version. Thesis Track does not operate a remote user account system for syncing your investment records.

If you delete the app, reset your device, or remove app data, your local records may be lost.

3. Notifications

Thesis Track may use local notifications to remind you when an investment thesis is due for review. Notifications are only used for reminders related to records you create in the app.

You can enable or disable notification permissions in your device settings.

4. Data Sharing

Thesis Track does not sell your personal data. In the current version, Thesis Track does not share your investment records with advertisers, data brokers, or third-party marketing services.

5. Analytics and Tracking

The current version of Thesis Track does not require third-party advertising tracking to function. If analytics or crash reporting tools are added in a future version, this Privacy Policy and the App Store privacy information will be updated accordingly.

6. Financial Disclaimer

Thesis Track is for personal investment journaling, learning, and review only. It does not provide financial advice, investment recommendations, price predictions, guaranteed returns, trading signals, or automated trading services. Investing involves risk. Please make your own decisions.

7. Children’s Privacy

Thesis Track is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can address the issue.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the effective date on this page.
